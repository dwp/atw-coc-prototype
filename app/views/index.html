Get help at work if you’re disabled or have a health condition (Access to Work)

Report a change of circumstances

If you’re getting support from Access to Work you need to report changes to:

  • your disability or health condition (physical or mental)
  • your home or work address - if you get travel to work support
  • your employer, job role or working pattern

You can report a change of circumstances online or by phone.

You will only need to provide your name, address, date of birth and what it is that's changed.

It would help if you could provide your unique reference number. You’ll find this on any correspondence we've sent you about your grant.

What happens when you report a change of circumstances

An Access to Work adviser will contact you to discuss the changes. They may request further information about your condition, depending on your circumstances. They may also contact your employer.

Report a change of circumstances by phone

You can report a change of circumstances by calling the Access to Work helpline. Make sure you have all the necessary details with you when you call.

Access to Work helpline

Telephone: 0800 121 7479
Textphone: 0800 121 7579

Alternative formats

Call the Access to Work number to ask for alternative formats, such as braille, large print or audio CD.

Complaints

You can contact Access to Work to complain if you are not happy with how your case has been handled.
